Color & Contrast

Description: Adjusts the visual properties of the media to expose data hidden in specific color channels or low-visibility areas.
How-to: Isolate color channels (Red/Green/Blue) or push Contrast/Brightness to the extremes to reveal faint steganographic "ghosts."

LSB Extraction i

LSB Extraction

Description: Scans the "Least Significant Bits" of pixels to find embedded binary data like text or file signatures.
How-to: Select the target bits (Bit 0 is most common) and click "Start Analysis" to parse the media for hidden payloads.
