TROUBLESHOOTING:
----------------

Problem: Corpus status shows "Fallback" even though I added documents
Solution: 
- Make sure text-stego-index.html is in the SAME domain/origin
- Check browser console for localStorage errors
- Click "Refresh" button to force reload
- Verify localStorage key 'crypticfox_corpus' exists (F12 → Application → Local Storage)

Problem: Suggestions don't seem to use corpus words
Solution:
- Corpus words must match pattern and length
- Context scoring is supplementary, not absolute
- Very rare words might rank lower despite being in corpus
- Add more documents to increase corpus word frequencies

Problem: Updates in text-stego-index don't appear in frequency.html
Solution:
- Click "Refresh" button (don't just reload page)
- Verify both pages are in same browser session
- Check localStorage size limits (typically 5-10MB)
